Privacy Policy

The Clearing collects no personal data. Zero trackers. Zero analytics. Zero cookies.

What We Don't Collect

  • No Google Analytics or tracking pixels
  • No cookies
  • No IP address logging
  • No email addresses (unless you voluntarily subscribe to the newsletter)
  • No browsing behavior data

Newsletter

If you subscribe to The Dispatch, your email address is stored with Formspree and used only to send you the weekly newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to any email.

Quiz & Interactive Tools

All interactive tools (the AI Fatigue Quiz, Check-in, Journal, etc.) store data locally in your browser using localStorage. We have no access to this data. It never leaves your device.

Share Your Story

Anonymous stories submitted via the Share Your Story form are reviewed and may be published with permission. No identifying information is stored unless explicitly provided.

Third-Party Links

Clearing-ai.com may link to external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The date of last update is shown at the bottom of this page.
