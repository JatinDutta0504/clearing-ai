About

A quiet place in a loud industry.

What this is

The Clearing is a small corner of the internet built for software engineers who need a moment. Not a productivity tool. Not a course. Not another thing to integrate into your workflow.

Just a place to breathe, write something that nobody reads, and remember that you're a person — not a pipeline.

You can use the breathing tool, dump some thoughts, write in your private journal, or just read the essay and feel less alone. Then go back. There's no pressure to stay.

Why it exists

A few engineers built this because they needed it and couldn't find it anywhere. The tech industry is very good at building tools that make you faster. It's not so good at making space for the humans inside the engineers.

AI changed the pace of work faster than anyone expected. The tools are remarkable. The burnout is also remarkable. Both things are true, and it felt worth acknowledging the second one out loud.

What we believe

  • 🌿 Slowness is not a bug. Humans are not supposed to run at machine speed.
  • 🌿 Your worth as an engineer is not your output. It never was.
  • 🌿 Rest is not a reward you earn. It's a condition required to do good work.
  • 🌿 The best code comes from rested minds with opinions, not from exhausted reviewers rubber-stamping AI output.
  • 🌿 Nobody should feel ashamed for being tired. Especially not right now.
  • 🌿 Your journal entries stay on your device. Your thoughts belong to you. We don't want your data.

"The goal was never to build something big.
It was to build something true."
