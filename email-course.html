Day 1 — The Middleman Problem

📬

From: The Clearing

Subject: Day 1 — You are not losing your mind

Hi [your name],

Welcome to the 5-day AI Fatigue Recovery Course.

Before we get into the practice, let's name what's actually happening — because what you're experiencing has a structure, and once you see the structure, it gets easier to work with.

The Middleman Problem: 71% of engineers in our survey reported regularly feeling like a middleman between the AI tool and the actual output. Not confidently shipping code — just curating responses. Reviewing. Editing. Explaining to the AI what you actually meant.

"I can get the code to work, but I can't tell you why it works without the AI layer. Six months ago I could have."

Your Day 1 practice: Open your last 10 PR descriptions. Count how many times you wrote something you don't fully understand yourself. That number is your middleman score — and it's the starting point for everything else.
