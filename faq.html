Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers about AI fatigue and what this site is about.

AI fatigue is a form of cognitive and emotional exhaustion specific to working with AI tools daily. It's characterized by: a sense of detachment from your own work, anxiety about professional relevance, mental fatigue from reviewing AI output, loss of confidence in your unaided judgment, and a kind of boredom even when you're shipping a lot. It's different from regular burnout in that it can occur even when workload is "normal" — the exhaustion comes from the nature of the work, not its volume.

It's not a clinical diagnosis — but neither is "burnout" in most formal senses, and nobody doubts that's real. AI fatigue is a recognizable pattern that thousands of engineers are experiencing and describing in similar ways. The phenomenon is real. Whether it has a DSM code is beside the point.
