Why This Exists

The short version

We built The Clearing after watching too many good engineers lose something they couldn't name — code ownership, craft satisfaction, the feeling that they're still learning and growing. The name of that thing is AI fatigue, and until recently, no one had written it down.

The data from 3,000+ quiz takers tells a consistent story: 63% feel like strangers to their own code. 58% report measurable skill decline. 71% take the quiz as a kind of test — to see if what they're feeling is real. It is.

If you're in crisis: If you're seriously considering leaving tech, or if anything you're feeling goes beyond fatigue into depression or hopelessness — please visit our mental health resources page. This site is about recovery, not crisis intervention. You deserve real support.

Don't try to fix everything. Pick the one thing from your tier's page that resonates most. The most cited tactic from 3,000+ quiz takers: The Explanation Requirement.

The Explanation Requirement

Before you accept any AI suggestion, write one sentence: "I added this because ___." No AI. Your words. This rebuilds the ownership loop that AI tools quietly dissolve.

Why it works: It forces completion of the cognitive circuit — input → judgment → output → ownership. AI skips the first three. You fill in the gap.
