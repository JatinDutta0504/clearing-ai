## Posting Guidelines

### Tone
- Honest, not preachy
- Data-backed but human
- No hustle culture noise
- Practical, not inspirational

### What to Post
✅ Survey data and real numbers
✅ Manager guides and practical tools
✅ Engineer stories (anonymized)
✅ Research-backed insights
❌ Don't post "just" content — lead with insight

### What NOT to Post
❌ No "growth hacking" or metrics humble-brag
❌ No productivity tips or hustle culture
❌ No AI-tool promotional content
❌ No engagement bait ("Comment YES if...")

### Frequency
- 3 posts per week minimum
- Daily engagement (15 min/day)
- Reply to all comments within 2 hours
