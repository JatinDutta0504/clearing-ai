### Post 3: The Retention Crisis (LEADER INSIGHT)
"AI fatigue is the retention crisis no one is talking about.

Your best engineers — the ones who care about craft, who take ownership, who notice the edge cases — are the ones most at risk.

Why? Because AI tools are most effective for repetitive, commodity work. The engineers who care most about depth and quality feel the skill erosion fastest.

They're watching themselves become prompt reviewers. They can't name it yet. But they're quietly updating their LinkedIn.

What you can do:
→ Name the phenomenon (AI fatigue is real)
→ Protect no-AI time for deep work
→ Have 1:1s where this is a safe topic
→ Share resources (clearing-ai.com has free guides)

The engineers who feel seen will stay. The ones who feel gaslit will leave.

What's your experience managing through this? 👇"
