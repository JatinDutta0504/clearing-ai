## Why Follow-Up Works

Cold email follow-up increases response rate by 40-60%.

**Rule:** 1 follow-up only. No third email.

**Subject line:** Re: AI fatigue resource for [publication name] readers + [one-line hook]

Thanks for your time,

## Warm Lead Pipeline (If Someone Responds)

### If they say YES:
"Amazing! Here are a few options:
1. I can write a 400-word original piece for your newsletter (your voice, your format)
2. I can give you the key stats + link to clearing-ai.com/recovery
3. I can provide a short quote + link for your next issue

What works best for you?"

### If they say NO:
"Thank you for considering it. Best of luck with [publication name]!"

### If they don't respond:
Don't email again. They're either busy or not interested.

## Timing

Send follow-ups on:
- **Tuesday or Wednesday** (Apr 9-10) — mid-week, people catch up on email
- **9:00 AM local time** of recipient's timezone
- OR **Tuesday 6:00 PM PDT** (evening inbox check)

Don't send Fridays (low engagement) or Mondays (inbox overload).
