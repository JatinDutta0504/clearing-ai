# AI Free Fridays — Strategy Document

## Concept
Encourage engineers to take Fridays off from AI tools entirely — not as a detox but as a skill-preservation practice. "AI-Free Fridays" is a behavior-change program, not a guilt trip.

## Positioning
- For engineers who use AI constantly and feel skill erosion
- Not "stop using AI" — more like "gym day for your coding brain"
- Counter-cultural (everyone pushes AI adoption, this pushes deliberate restraint)
- Practical, positive framing: what you GAIN, not what you lose

## Page Structure (ai-free-fridays.html)
- Hero: "The Friday Experiment" — what if every Friday you coded without AI?
- Why this works: cognitive workout framing (gym analogy)
- What happens in your brain: retrieval practice, skill maintenance
- How to frame it to your team: not anti-AI, pro-craft
- What to do with the time: project Euler, OSS contributions, code reviews, reading
- The 4-week challenge: start week 1 (2 hours), build to full Friday
