**The one tactic that compounds:**

Every week we ask subscribers: "What's the one change that made the most difference?"

This week's answer, again and again: the Explanation Requirement.

Not because it's complicated. Because it reveals the gap instantly.

If you can't explain why the AI's code works, you didn't learn anything from shipping it. That's not a character flaw. That's a signal. And the signal is useful.

Try it on one function today. Explain it out loud. Write down what you don't know. That's the beginning of the recovery loop.
