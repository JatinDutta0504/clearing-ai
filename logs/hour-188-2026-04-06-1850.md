## Why This Page Was Built

The site had 98 pages but no dedicated content for freelance/independent engineers — despite this being one of the highest-risk demographics for AI fatigue. Freelancers face:
- No team feedback loop (no one notices when you're deteriorating)
- Income precarity compounding the income-fatique trap
- Client-mandated AI tooling with no negotiation leverage
- Isolation amplifying automation anxiety
- No institutional structures for recovery

This is a genuinely new vertical that complements remote-work-ai-fatigue.html (which focuses on async/distributed teams) with a focus on the solo practitioner, independent contractor, and freelance engineer experience.
