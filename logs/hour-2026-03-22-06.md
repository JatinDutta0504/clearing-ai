## What was built

**New page: `recovery.html` — "How to Recover from AI Fatigue: A Practical Guide for Engineers"**

The single most strategically important page built this overnight session. Recovery-intent queries are the highest-converting search traffic: people who already know what they have and are looking for solutions. This page is built to rank for those queries while being genuinely, substantively useful.

### Structure

8 named sections:
1. **What you're actually dealing with** — frames the 4 dimensions of AI fatigue (cognitive erosion, identity displacement, decision fatigue, ownership anxiety). Sets up the reader's mental model before any advice.
2. **The 7 phases of recovery** — Acknowledgment → Stopping the bleeding → Inventory → Deliberate reclamation → Reconnecting with ownership → Sustainable integration → Maintenance. Concrete, sequential, non-linear-but-directional.
3. **Day by day timeline** — 5 timeline nodes: Days 1–3, Week 1, Weeks 2–3, Months 1–2, Month 3+. Honest expectations, not marketing copy.
4. **Interactive recovery checklist** — 11 actions, localStorage persistence, progress dots, contextual score messages. Fully vanilla JS. Returns to your progress on next visit.
5. **Specific strategies by dimension** — 4 strategy blocks (cognitive erosion, identity displacement, decision fatigue, ownership anxiety) each with 3–4 concrete, named techniques.
6. **4 recovery traps** — The cold turkey overcorrection, waiting until ready, treating it as purely individual, measuring recovery by productivity.
7. **When it's more than fatigue** — Mental health escalation path, honest acknowledgment of when this isn't enough.
8. **FAQ accordion** — 6 questions with full answers: timeline, cold turkey, fastest first step, changing jobs, AI fatigue vs burnout, recovering in a team that uses AI constantly.

### Interactive features
- **Recovery checklist** with `localStorage` — checks persist across visits. Progress dots. Contextual encouragement messages at different thresholds.
- **FAQ accordion** — spring-eased open/close, ARIA expanded states, keyboard accessible.
- **Table of contents** — smooth-scroll to sections, styled subtly.
- **Dark mode** — full dark theme via `[data-theme="dark"]` inherited from `main.js`, all custom properties covered in page-level styles.
