### Page Structure

**The 12 mental models:**
1. **The Scaffolding Test** — Does AI help you build something you can maintain without it?
2. **The 80/20 Inversion** — Are you spending cognitive energy on the 20% that matters?
3. **The Ownership Ledger** — What in this codebase do you own well enough to be accountable for?
4. **The Muscle Memory Test** — If AI disappeared tomorrow, which skills would still be sharp?
5. **First Principles First** — Have you thought about the problem before asking AI about it?
6. **The Calibration Loop** — How accurate is your model of what AI can and can't do well?
7. **The Cognitive Budget** — Are you protecting the cognitive capacity needed for what matters most?
8. **The Discomfort Signal** — Are you avoiding productive struggle you should sit with?
9. **Zones of Practice** — Are you actively protecting time for real work without AI?
10. **The Identity Anchor** — Are you still an engineer, or have you become an AI manager?
11. **The Explanation Requirement** — Can you explain this fully to a colleague without the AI?
12. **The Long Game** — Is how you're using AI building the engineer you want to be in 5 years?
