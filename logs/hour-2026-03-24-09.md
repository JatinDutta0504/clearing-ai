### Content Structure (~4,700 words)

**6 Pillars:**
1. **Sleep** — 7–8 hours essential, 23-min recovery stat from Gloria Mark research, room temperature 65–68°F, schedule consistency
2. **Nutrition** — Stable blood sugar, protein+fat+fiber at every meal, caffeine strategy (1 coffee 90 min after waking, nothing after 2 PM)
3. **Movement** — 30 min daily (walking counts), BDNF boost, cortisol reduction, measurable cognitive improvement within weeks
4. **Deep Work** — Protecting 2–4 hour uninterrupted blocks, async communication windows, calendar defense strategies, manager conversation scripts
5. **Relationships** — At least 1 real human connection per week as immunity buffer against isolation, hobby communities, honest vulnerability
6. **Career Design** — Quarterly alignment checks (4 questions: am I learning? values alignment? autonomy? sustainable pace?), exit signals, staying vs leaving framework

**Supporting Sections:**
- 4 card grids per pillar with actionable insights, research backing, warning signs, and quick wins
