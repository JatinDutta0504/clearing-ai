**The fix pattern (before):**
```html
<div class="footer-bottom">
  <p>© 2026 The Clearing. Built for engineers, by engineers.</p>
</div>
</div>          ← orphan close — REMOVED
```

```html
</div>
        <a href="hn-launch.html" style="font-size:13px;color:var(--text2);">HN Launch</a>
```
