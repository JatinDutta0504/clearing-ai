## This Window: Phase 4 — Email Confirmation Welcome Experience

### The Problem
When engineers subscribe via Formspree, they land on `confirmed.html` — a basic success page. It doesn't reinforce the community connection, show them what to expect from The Dispatch, or give them immediate next steps. The confirmation moment is wasted.

### The Fix
Build `welcome.html` — a rich welcome experience for new newsletter subscribers. This replaces/augments the confirmed.html redirect. Features:
1. **Personalized greeting** — "You're part of [X]k engineers recovering from AI fatigue"
2. **What The Dispatch delivers** — 4 clear promises, 1-min read, weekly
3. **Most recent issue preview** — shows what they'll get (links to Dispatch #31)
4. **Quick-start resources** — 3 immediately actionable things (take quiz, try no-AI block, read stories)
5. **Community connection** — testimonials section, link to community-hub
6. **Email course teaser** — "Check your inbox for Day 1 of the 5-Day Reset"
7. **Share the mission** — Twitter/LinkedIn share buttons with pre-written copy
8. **Dark + light mode**, full accessibility, schema (WebPage + BreadcrumbList)

### Content Structure (~2,200 words)

**Section 1: Welcome Header**
- Large personalized message: "You found The Clearing"
- Stats badge: "Join [X,XXX]+ engineers on the recovery path"
- Subheading: "Your first issue of The Dispatch arrives [day]. Here's what to expect."

**Section 2: What You Just Signed Up For**
- 4 Dispatch promises (bullet cards):
  - 📊 Real data, not productivity theater
  - 🧠 Science-backed recovery tactics
  - 🤝 Stories from engineers who understand
  - 💡 One actionable thing per week
- "The Dispatch is 1 email per week. Read time: under 5 minutes."

**Section 3: Your First Step — Take the Quiz**
- Quiz CTA with tier badge preview
- "8 questions, 4 dimensions, one number that explains what's happening to you"
- Link to `ai-fatigue-severity-index.html`

**Section 4: While You Wait — Try These Today**
- 3 action cards (interactive, clickable):
  - 🌿 No-AI Block (30 min, rebuild muscle memory) → links to no-ai-block.html
  - 📖 Read One Story (anonymous engineer, deeply relatable) → links to stories.html
  - 🧘 Decompress (ambient sounds, breathing, 10 min) → links to decompress.html

**Section 5: Recent Dispatch Preview**
- Preview of Dispatch #31 (the most recent issue)
- Headline + 2-sentence summary + "Read the full issue →" link
- Shows the quality of what they'll receive

**Section 6: 5-Day Reset Course**
- "Check your inbox — Day 1 of the 5-Day AI Fatigue Reset arrives tonight"
- Brief description of the email course arc
- Link to email-course.html for course outline

**Section 7: Community Stories (3 testimonials)**
- 3 quote cards from testimonials (tier badges)
- Link to testimonials-campaign.html for full collection

**Section 8: Share The Clearing**
- "Know an engineer who needs this? Share The Clearing."
- Twitter + LinkedIn share buttons with pre-written copy
- Copy-link button

**Section 9: FAQ**
- "How often do you email?" → "Once a week, every Monday morning"
- "Can I unsubscribe?" → "One-click unsubscribe, always"
- "Will you spam me?" → "No. We send The Dispatch and occasional breaking resources. Nothing else."
