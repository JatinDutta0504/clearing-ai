## This Window: Dispatch Newsletter Archive — 5 Key Issues Built

### Why This Matters

The newsletter is The Clearing's primary retention and referral channel. Every issue that currently links to "Subscribe to read →" is a dead end for:
1. Existing subscribers clicking archive links in emails
2. New visitors exploring the newsletter from the archive page
3. Search engines crawling the archive

Building real dispatch HTML pages for the most important issues creates actual reading experiences instead of subscription CTAs.
