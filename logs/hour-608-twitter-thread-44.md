The weird thing about the last 2 years of AI-assisted coding:

**Tweet 2/9**

Before AI: I'd struggle for 4 hours, hit a wall, Google 30 min, aha moment, code, remember it forever.

Nothing sticks.

We're running on a treadmill where the belt never stops.
And calling it productivity.

→ Ship features weekly, couldn't explain how the system works
→ Genuinely lost without AI — not just inconvenienced
