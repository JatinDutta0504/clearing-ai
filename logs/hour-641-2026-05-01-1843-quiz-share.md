## Decision: Focus on Viral Share Loop

The quiz is the primary entry point for organic traffic. With no email list and broken forms, the fastest growth lever is **turning quiz takers into viral amplifiers**.

The quiz-results.html already has share buttons, but:
1. No dedicated share-gateway page (hub for all sharing paths)
2. No share-to-unlock mechanism (bonus content for sharing)
3. No QR code for in-person sharing
4. Pre-written copy not prominent enough

**Building `quiz-share.html`:** A dedicated viral share page that:
- Takes tier parameter (?tier=1-4)
- Shows share buttons with pre-written viral copy prominently
- Has share-to-unlock: "Share this to get the full AI Fatigue Recovery PDF"
- QR code generator for conference/in-person sharing
- Email capture that uses the mailto fallback (works even with broken Formspree)
- Social proof: "Join 2,847 engineers who shared this"
