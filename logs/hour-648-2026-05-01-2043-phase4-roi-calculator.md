## What's Being Built This Window

### Asset 1: AI Fatigue ROI Calculator (`ai-fatigue-roi-calculator.html`)
A standalone interactive tool — quantifies the real cost of AI fatigue for engineers and teams.

**Why this matters:**
- Engineers: "Is AI fatigue actually hurting my career/salary?" — concrete numbers
- Managers/CTOs: Build business case for addressing AI fatigue
- HR/People teams: Quantify the cost of ignoring the problem
- **Monetization lever:** ROI calculator results make the cost undeniable → drives people toward paid offerings

**Calculator dimensions:**
1. **Skill Atrophy Cost** — hours/month in reduced coding ability × your hourly rate
2. **Productivity Theater Cost** — extra review cycles × time wasted × hourly rate
3. **Turnover Risk** — probability of leaving × replacement cost ($50k-$150k typical)
4. **Recruitment Cost** — difficulty hiring × recruiter fees × team size
5. **Wellbeing Cost** — sleep impact × health risk × insurance cost
6. **Total Monthly Cost** — sum of all dimensions

**Output:** Monthly cost + Annual cost + "What 30 days of recovery costs" + "What ignoring it costs" + comparison

**Design:** Dark mode, single-page tool, shareable results, CTA to newsletter/program
