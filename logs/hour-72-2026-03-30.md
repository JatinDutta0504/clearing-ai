## Critical Fix: Blocking Google Fonts on research.html

**Problem identified:** research.html (the 4th highest traffic page) had a standard blocking `<link>` for Google Fonts causing LCP of 3.7s (Google threshold: <2.5s).

**Google Fonts audit across all 60 pages:**
- 54 pages: Already had `media="print" onload="this.media='all'"` non-render-blocking pattern ✅
- 6 pages still had blocking `<link>` tags ❌
  - research.html (critical — was 3.7s LCP FAIL)
  - about.html
  - community.html
  - sitemap.html
  - stories.html
  - 404.html

**Fix applied:** Python script replaced blocking `<link>` with:
```html
<link href="...fonts.googleapis.com..." rel="stylesheet" media="print" onload="this.media='all'" />
<noscript><link rel="stylesheet" href="...fonts.googleapis.com..." /></noscript>
```

**Expected post-fix LCP:** research.html: 3.7s → 1.5s (Google Fonts will load asynchronously)
