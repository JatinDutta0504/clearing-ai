## Day-14 Strategy: Final, Warm, No Pressure

Day-14 is the last touch. The tone shifts to:
- Genuine warmth (not "please, please")
- One clear last offer (free partnership, any time)
- Implicit: "I won't ping you again after this"
- Slight vulnerability: acknowledge this is a last message
