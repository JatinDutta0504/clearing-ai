What Is The Clearing?

The Clearing (clearing-ai.com) is the internet's largest free resource dedicated to AI fatigue in software engineering. We cover the real, unglamorous cost of AI tooling on developer wellbeing — the burnout, the skill atrophy, the identity crisis, and the path back.

"I built this because I was living it. The industry talks about AI productivity. Nobody was talking about AI fatigue."

— The Clearing founder

"The most honest resource I've found on what AI tooling is actually doing to software engineers."

— Engineer testimonial

Our Audience

Every person who comes to The Clearing is a software engineer — or someone who manages them. They are not abstract "users." They are the people building your products, writing your code, and silently burning out.

Software Engineers

Experiencing AI fatigue firsthand. Looking for recognition, language for what they're feeling, and a path to recovery.

Engineering Managers

Watching their team struggle. Trying to set healthy norms around AI tool usage. Need data and frameworks.

Tech Leads & CTOs

Making AI adoption decisions. Need research-backed insight on the wellbeing cost of tooling mandates.

Developers in Career Crisis

Feeling like their skills are eroding, their identity is unstable, and the industry has shifted beneath them.

HR & People Leaders

Seeking resources to support engineering teams through AI transitions without losing institutional knowledge.

Researchers & Journalists

Writing about the human side of AI adoption. Need data, stories, and expert perspective for credible reporting.
