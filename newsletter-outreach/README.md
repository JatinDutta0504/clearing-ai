## What We're Offering

**Option A: Sponsored Slot**
- Brief sponsor message in one issue (~75 words)
- CTA: "Take the AI Fatigue Quiz" + link
- Cost: Free (we're a free resource, just want reach)

**Option B: Co-Promotion**
- We write a Dispatch issue featuring their newsletter
- They feature our quiz/stats in one issue
- Both audiences get introduced

**Option C: Content Mention**
- Simply mention our stats page or quiz in a relevant article
- No cost, no coordination needed
- Perfect for TLDR/Bytes style "here's a useful resource" mentions
