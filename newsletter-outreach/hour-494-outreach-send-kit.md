## Pro Tips

1. **Personalize the first line** — reference something specific about their newsletter if you can
2. **Send Tuesday or Wednesday** — highest open rates for dev newsletters
3. **Keep subject lines under 50 chars** — mobile preview is everything
4. **Track ?ref= parameter** — so you know which partnership drove traffic when they visit
5. **One ask, clearly** — either co-promotion or a link. Don't bury the ask.
