All 8 tools, at a glance

Every tool is free, private (localStorage only), and built for engineers by engineers.

🎯

AI Fatigue Quiz

Find out where you stand

5 questions. 2 minutes. Gives you a tier score (🌿–🌑) with personalized recommendations for your exact level of fatigue.

Best for: First visit, or checking in after recovery work.
⏱ 2 min ✓ Free
📝

Daily Check-in

Your 30-second daily readout

Answer 5 questions about today's energy, focus, mood, and AI usage. Track your patterns over time with a 30-day dot calendar and past-entry review.

Best for: Building self-awareness. Takes 30 seconds. Do it every morning.
⏱ 30 sec/day ✓ Free
📓

Recovery Journal

Reflect, name, process

8 structured reflective prompts for processing AI fatigue experiences: naming the loss, identifying triggers, tracking patterns, and reconnecting with craft.

Best for: When you're processing a hard week or reconnecting with why you became an engineer.
⏱ 5–15 min ✓ Free
🍅

Deep Work Timer

Reclaim your focus

A Pomodoro timer built for deep work. 25-min focus / 5-min break cycles with ambient sound options, session tracking, and long-break reminders after 4 sessions.

Best for: Rebuilding attention capacity. Start with one session. Build up to 4.
⏱ 25–90 min ✓ Free
📊

Fatigue Severity Index

Score your fatigue precisely

10-question clinical-style assessment with weighted scoring across 4 dimensions: cognitive load, emotional exhaustion, skill erosion, and identity loss.

Best for: A detailed picture of where your fatigue is coming from. Good before starting the 30-day plan.
⏱ 5 min ✓ Free
🧠

Fatigue Type Calculator

What's driving your fatigue?

Not all AI fatigue is the same. This calculator identifies your primary fatigue archetype: Cognitive Overloader, Identity Eroder, Skill Atrophier, or Velocity Addict.

Best for: Understanding the root cause of your fatigue so you can target the right recovery practices.
⏱ 3 min ✓ Free
✅

30-Day Recovery Checklist

Your structured recovery plan

A month-long structured protocol across 4 phases: Awareness, Reduction, Reconnection, and Integration. 30 concrete daily actions with localStorage progress tracking and a print-ready version.

Best for: Engineers who want a structured plan. Works best if you do one action per day.
⏱ 10–20 min/day ✓ Free
💬

The Explanation Requirement

Rebuild the learning loop

Before accepting any AI-generated code, explain it out loud — in plain English — as if you're teaching someone. If you can't explain it, you don't own it yet.

Best for: Daily practice. One of the most effective interventions for skill atrophy and identity recovery.
⏱ 5 min/session ✓ Free
