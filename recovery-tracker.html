What to log — and why

Fatigue level: Your overall AI-mediated exhaustion — Sunday night dread, code middleman feeling, struggling to start.

Symptoms: AI fatigue shows up in specific ways. Tracking which ones hit hardest helps you spot triggers.

AI usage vs. practice: The most powerful insight comes from correlating what you did with how you felt.

Consistency beats intensity. A 3-minute log every Sunday evening is worth more than a detailed audit once a month.

FAQ

Is my data private?▼
Yes. Everything is stored in your browser localStorage — not on any server. Export JSON to back up before clearing.
How long until I see patterns?▼
Most engineers see a meaningful trend around weeks 3–4. With 6+ weeks, correlations become clear.
Does this replace the AI Fatigue Quiz?▼
No — the quiz gives a one-time severity score. The tracker measures week-to-week trajectory. Use both.
What counts as a "no-AI block"?▼
Any deliberate 30+ minute stretch where you solve a problem without AI. The point is productive struggle.
Can I export my data?▼
Yes. Export JSON downloads your full log. Your data is always yours.
What if I miss a week?▼
Log it anyway. Even with gaps, the trend is meaningful.
