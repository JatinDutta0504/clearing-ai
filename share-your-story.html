Why It Matters

Naming the feeling is the first step to recovering from it

Most engineers experiencing AI fatigue have never said it out loud. They don't have the vocabulary, and they assume they're alone. Reading that someone else felt exactly the same thing — and found a way through — changes everything.

🗣

You Name What Nobody Can Name

AI fatigue is still unnamed in most workplaces. Your story adds to a growing vocabulary that helps people recognize what's happening to them.

💡

What Helped You Might Help Them

The tactics that pulled you through — no-AI blocks, Explanation Requirement, therapy, quitting — are tactics another engineer hasn't tried yet.

🤝

Isolation Is the Core Wound

AI fatigue makes engineers feel broken. Every shared story chips away at that isolation. It says: this is not a personal failure. It's a structural condition.

📚

It Makes the Site More Real

Every authentic story on The Clearing makes it easier for the next engineer to trust the resource and actually use it.

How to Share

Three ways to tell your story

Choose what feels safest and most authentic to you.

🔒 Fully Anonymous

We'll publish your story with no identifying information. We won't ask your name, email, or employer. We may edit for length and clarity.

Most common

🏷 First Name + Role Only

"Sarah, Senior IC at a Series B startup." We won't share your last name or anything that makes you easily searchable.

Optional

📝 Write It Yourself

Send us your draft as a Google Doc or email body. We'll publish it as-is with any edits you request. Full editorial control stays with you.

Editorial

💬 Just a Quote

Don't have a full story? A single paragraph or even a sentence about what AI fatigue felt like is enough to matter.

Short-form

Our Commitment

What we publish — and what we don't

✅ We Publish

Anonymous stories with no identifying details. First-name-only attributions. Full drafts you write yourself. Single powerful quotes. Stories from engineers at any tier — early fatigue to severe burnout.

❌ We Don't Publish

Names of specific employers without permission. Content that attacks specific individuals. Medical advice. Anything submitted in bad faith to harm the community.

🔒 Your Privacy

We never sell or share your submission data. Your email is only used to follow up about your story. You can request removal at any time.

✏️ Our Editing

We may trim for length, fix obvious typos, or clarify ambiguous phrasing. We won't change the meaning or tone of your story. You'll always have final say.
