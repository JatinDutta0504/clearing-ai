Why Share a Badge?

Most engineers are suffering in silence. Your badge gives them permission to say something.

Putting it into words — "AI fatigue is real" — is the first step toward doing something about it. Sharing publicly is a commitment to your own recovery, and to the people on your team who are quietly struggling.

When one engineer shares this, it gives everyone else permission to say "actually, me too." That's how culture changes — not from a Slack message nobody replies to, but from one person being brave enough to name it first.
